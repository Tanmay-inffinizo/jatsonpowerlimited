01 — Solutions

Turnkey Project Electrification

03 — Retrofit

Switchboard Retro Fitting

25+ years of expertise in upgrading aging circuits and switchgear to modern electronic trip units for enhanced protection coordination.

04 — Audit

Energy Conservation & Audits

Authorized Gujarat State electrical and thermal audits using modern diagnostic tools to provide viable energy-saving solutions.

05 — Maintenance

LV & MV Maintenance

Specialized shutdown maintenance, busbar inspection, and air circuit breaker servicing to ensure zero failures during peak loads.

06 — Digitalize

Plant / Factory Digitalization

Transitioning facilities to Industry 4.0 through IoT/IIoT platforms and the implementation of intelligent iMCC Switchboards.

07 — Quality

Power Quality Solutions

Comprehensive audits to correct power factor to unity and mitigate voltage sags, ensuring clean power and lower electricity bills.

08 — Analyze

Harmonics Audits

Detailed analysis of VFD-induced distortions to size APFCs/AHFs, ensuring the health of sensitive semiconductor components.

09 — SaaS

Energy Analytics (EMAS)

ISO 50001-based SaaS platform for real-time energy monitoring, analytics, and reporting via integrated IoT software.

10 — Servicing

Switchgear & Panel Servicing

Expert cleaning, lubrication, and wiring simulation for all panel cubicles to avoid hazardous conditions and financial losses.

11 — Prevent

Preventive Maintenance

Routine scheduled maintenance for transformers and substation equipment to reduce chances of costly unplanned machine downtime.
