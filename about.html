Our Journey

Company
Legacy

From a focused electrical contractor to a full-spectrum industrial electrification partner — the JATSON story spans over two decades of growth, trust, and technical mastery.

2004

Company Incorporation

JATSON Electrical Systems is founded in Valsad, Gujarat, as a turnkey electrical contracting firm serving local industry.

2009

LV Switchboard Manufacturing

Expansion into in-house manufacturing of Low Voltage Switchboards, reducing dependency on external suppliers and improving quality control.

2015

MV Division Launch

Launch of the Medium Voltage Switchboard division, enabling JATSON to serve larger industrial and infrastructure clients.

2020

Energy Audit Services

Introduction of certified Energy Audit services, helping industrial clients optimise consumption and achieve sustainability targets.
